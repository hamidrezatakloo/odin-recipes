Cheesy ramen noodles

Mix American cheese with tasty ramen noodles for a simple, low-budget meal

ingredients
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese
directions
  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended
